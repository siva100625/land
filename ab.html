Introduction to the Northern Lights



       The Northern Lights, also known as Aurora Borealis, are a natural light display
       predominantly seen in the high-latitude regions around the Arctic Circle. 
       They occur when charged particles from the sun collide with atoms in the
       Earth's atmosphere,creating a stunning array of colors, including green,
       pink, purple, and blue. These ethereal curtains of light dance across 
       the night sky, captivating observers with their beauty and mystery.

Fairbanks: A Symphony of Light Beneath the Auroral Oval"




    Fairbanks, Alaska, USA: Fairbanks, located in the interior of Alaska,
    is another popular destination for viewing the Northern Lights. 
    Its location beneath the "Auroral Oval" makes it one of the best places
    in the United States to witness this phenomenon.

 What is a webpage?

      A webpage is a document that is suitable for the World Wide Web and web browsers. It can contain text,
      images, videos, links, and interactive elements.


How is a webpage different from a website?

    A webpage is a single document, while a website is a collection of related webpages usually connected 
    through hyperlinks.
